About

I'm Todd — a traveler with a camera.

For years I've been drawn to the quiet moments between destinations: the way light falls across a quiet street at dawn, the color of a distant horizon, the texture of a place lived in.

This site is a collection of those moments. Right now the focus is travel, but expect portraits, landscapes, and more to follow.

If you'd like to reach out about a print, a trip, or just to chat — the contact page is open.
